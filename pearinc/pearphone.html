At a glance







Performance

Pearphone is equipped with everything you need.
From long battery life and snappy processors to large storage space
and wireless compatibility.

Long battery life
Pearphone learns your daily routine and power
down apps you don't use for maximum efficiency.
Make use of the improved power saving mode
to use of the 4100mAh battery to the fullest.
Snappy processors
With a Snapdragon 845 processor, enjoy using
the 12GB RAM to help your phone run smoothly when
playing graphic-heavy games.

Large storage space
Delete what you want to, not because you have to.
With an internal storage of 1TB on top of a 512GB
microSD card gives a revolutionary 1.5TB of storage
Fast wireless charging
With a wireless charging compatibility, simply
place your phone on a wireless charger and let it charge
your phone at a 20% increased rate.



Design

The next-generation smartphone,
designed to not just stand out, but stand apart


Crystal clear display
With the latest AMOLED screen, expect top-tier real
color and contrast in each scene - including dark ones
Make use of the brighter screen for use in bright daylight
and the blue-light filter at night for eye comfort
Fingerprint scanner
With a built-in fingerprint scanner, it makes it so that
only you can access your phone. Secure and convinient,
with just a quick tap you can hop straight into using it.

Materials selected for more comfort
With a nice glossy look with vibrant prismatic colors, on
top of a durable and comfortable ceramic finish makes
for an easy-to-handle phone. The Gorilla Glass 6 display
makes PearPhone withstand the rigours of daily usage.
IP68 Water and Dust Resistance
With an IP68 water and dust resistant, you don't have
to get worried of your PearPhone when you drop it in
water. Being able to resist up to 1.5m of depth for 30 mins,
never get punished for your mistakes again.




Camera

The next-generation camera,
Shoot pictures like never before


Adjustable scenes
Take any scene to suit your liking by manually
adjusting the settings such as ISO, shutter speed
and exposure level to your heart's content.
Shot suggestion
You don't need to be an expert to take a shot. Use
the ever helpful shot suggestion to take the best shot
you need.

4k HDR and HDR10+ Recording
Record incredible footage with amazing details with
the rich color and contrast of HDR10+ and Dynamic
Tone Mapping. Every frame stays consistent with the next.
Various wide angle cameras
With a wide choice of 12 MP telephoto, wide angle
and Ultra Wide camera, be sure to catch every angle
of the scene.




Pop-up Front Facing Camera
Sick of notches taking up you screen space?
With a 20MP pop-up front facing camera, enjoy
your clean display to the fullest. The camera will
pop-up smoothly when the camera app is open.
